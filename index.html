How Skill Trade Works

Skill trading is the lifeblood of off-grid living. Vanlifers swap services without currency exchanging hands.

For example, trade a solar wiring repair for wood paneling assistance or mechanical diagnostics for website build advice. Tap "Message Trader" to strike up a trade plan.
